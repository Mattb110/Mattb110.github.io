307 Fiddlers Court, Pickering

$1,700,000

4 bedroom 4 bathroom

Description

Welcome to 307 Fiddlers Court, an exceptional 4+1 bedroom executive home with four washrooms situated on a quiet street of west Pickering’s exclusive Rougemount neighbourhood.

Set back nicely from the street, this beautiful brick home showcases extensive stone and rock landscaping - including an interlocking driveway, tiered walkway, and steps; as well as a covered front entrance with an oversized entry door and frosted glass sidelights - adding to the home’s curb appeal.

Beyond the front door is an incredibly spacious foyer finished with upgraded oversized floor tiles that flow throughout the main hall, the powder room, and the laundry room. The foyer also features wainscoting, decorative crown moldings, and pot lighting - with the latter two design details also seen in the open concept living and dining rooms.

The second, third, and fourth bedrooms all feature double closets - and the main 4-pc washroom has a tiled floor and bath area, as well as a vanity with a quartz counter.

Through a double door entrance is the very generously sized master suite. Bathed in light through multiple south-facing windows, this exceptional retreat showcases a huge sitting area that overlooks the backyard, and upgraded light fixtures. Along the short hall to its ensuite washroom is a walk-in closet - while the ensuite itself showcases pot lighting and upgraded tiled finishes throughout;

Minutes to Dunbarton High and St. Mary Catholic Secondary Schools; as well as a short walk to St. Monica Catholic Elementary and Elizabeth B. Phin Public Schools - this home is also just a short drive to the Pickering Town Centre Mall, the GO Station, and has easy access to Hwy 401 - as well as being close to the shopping, entertainment, and waterfront enjoyment of Pickering’s beautiful Frenchman’s Bay community along Lake Ontario.
